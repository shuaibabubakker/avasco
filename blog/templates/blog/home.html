About Us

Welcome to Avasco, the Arts and Sports Cultural Organisation at Aviyoor! Avasco offer a broad spectrum of activities for individuals and various interest groups on our area. Avasco is recognized and registered under Nehru Yuva Kendra youth clubs, which focus on the organising competitive and social programs.

Our Mission

To develop young people in their physical, mental and emotional well being, so that they grow to full maturity as individuals and responsible members of society.

Our Vision

To deliver an exciting and innovative program of activities within the safe environment of the youth center. Encouraging young people to develop their social awareness, self esteem and skills for life, whilst having fun.
